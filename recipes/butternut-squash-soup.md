> Recipe adapted from the Guild Wars 2 cookbook —
> [Bowl of Sweet and Spicy Butternut Squash Soup](https://wiki.guildwars2.com/wiki/Bowl_of_Sweet_and_Spicy_Butternut_Squash_Soup).

A warming bowl of butternut squash soup with a sweet apple edge and a serious
chilli kick, built entirely from scratch. Adjust the ghost peppers down unless
you mean it.

## Steps

1. Make a roux: melt one stick of butter in a heavy pot, whisk in the bag of flour, and cook gently until smooth and pale gold.
2. Make the stock: simmer the carrots, onions, slab of poultry meat, and jug of water for 20–30 minutes, then strain out the solids.
3. Combine the roux, strained stock, glass of buttermilk, packet of salt, and black peppercorn, whisking into a smooth cream soup base.
4. Peel and cube the butternut squash, then simmer it in the base with the remaining stick of butter and the cayenne pepper until tender; blend until silky.
5. Grind the nutmeg seeds, cinnamon sticks, ginger root, and cloves together into a fresh pumpkin pie spice.
6. Stir the spice blend into the soup and let it bloom for a minute until fragrant.
7. Core and dice the apples; wearing gloves, finely mince the ghost peppers and add a little at a time, tasting as you go.
8. Fold in the diced apples and simmer for 5 minutes so they soften but keep some bite, then ladle into a bowl and serve hot.
